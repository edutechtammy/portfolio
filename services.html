Production and Project Management

What We Do

At Filament Games, our approach to project management emphasizes communication and full transparency. At the contract level, we will document a specific set of Release Criteria; the mutually agreed-upon conditions needed for a Release to be Accepted. The formal Acceptance process for each Release will be detailed in each Statement of Work and will be facilitated using Jira, an industry-leading online project management and collaboration tool that allows us to document and track all Release Criteria and development tasks.

Why We Do It So Well

Clients have access to Jira during implementation so that they have up-to-the-minute insight into our progress at all times. We’ll formally meet every two weeks for a Sprint review, during which we’ll discuss progress to date. Our teams are also available for adhoc meetings and communications on a daily basis. See below for details on the capabilities we use most in Jira as well as information about how we use the information generated by Jira to manage each project.

Design

What We Do

Our game designers work exclusively on design and documentation for playful, positive impact experiences. They are also the team’s lead client advocate. As client advocate, the designer translates and represents your goals as they move throughout the design and development process.

Why We Do It So Well

We work on extremely unique and innovative learning game ideas that require an additional layer of learning design work not required by consumer game genres. The design team has a long history of producing award-winning learning titles that meet these objectives.

Web Development

What We Do

Filament’s web development team specializes in taking your project online. Composed of experts in graphic design, front end web design, and back end web development and infrastructure, this team works flexibly across browsers and platforms to create a web experience that is elegant, usable, and intuitive. We work primarily in HTML5, and are versed in front end frameworks including Angular, React, and Vue, as well as front end languages including Typescript, and Javascript. For back end web work, we specialize in FastAPI, Flask, Django, PHP, Node, and Javascript.

Why We Do It Well

Our web development team has a long track record of shipping web-based projects that support the educational and impact goals of our clients. Because we staff back end, front end, and graphic design specialists, our clients benefit from end-to-end web development services that can cover everything from nuts-and-bolts SQL database implementation to flashy, graphics-rich progressive web apps. Fluent in mobile and browser technologies, our web team can also work in tandem with our game developers to create and integrate the web-based components of your game project.

Visual Interaction Design

What We Do

Each development team at Filament has a dedicated visual interaction designer who is responsible for designing the look and feel of each project. They also serve as the technical liaison between programmers and the illustration team.

Why We Do It Well

The visual interaction team is constantly iterating towards usability, clarity and quality in all player interactions. They specialize in creating experiences that eliminate unnecessary complexity, reduce the number of steps it takes to perform any given function, and reduce on-screen clutter so that the player has the tools and visual information they need to succeed.

Illustration and 3D Modeling

What We Do

The illustration team is used as a flexible resource across all projects. They are allocated to apply their breadth of styles and skill sets to each project in the studio as illustration demands rise and fall.

Why We Do It Well

Our game art team is broadly versed in all modes and styles of digital art, from retro pixel art to custom fully-rigged 3D models. With thoughtful considerations towards tone, mood, age range, and subject matter accuracy, our illustrators and 3D artists work diligently to ensure a pleasing aesthetic experience that suits your content, audience and budget.

Sound

What We Do

Our Sound team provides custom voice acting, narration, sound effects, and soundtrack compositions. This team also provides video production support for deliverables like in-game cinematics and promotional trailers.

Why We Do It Well

Filament provides comprehensive sound design and sound effect services. We produce songs in a vast variety of styles to fit the aesthetic of any game. Clients benefit from this capability by receiving wholly-owned custom content that does not require any third-party rights management.
